STAR GYM: A 20-YEAR FITNESS LEGACY!


Unleash Your Potential with World-Class Facilities and Expert Trainers


At STAR Gym, we believe in empowering your fitness journey with our internationally certified personal trainers, state-of-the-art equipment, and a welcoming environment. Our newly renovated 10,000 Sq. Ft. gym floor is equipped with top-tier brands like Life Fitness, Nautilus, Cybex, and Matrix, ensuring a premium workout experience.


Diverse Workouts for Every Fitness Level

Whether you’re a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, our over 150 group fitness classes, including Yoga, Zumba, and Kickboxing, cater to every preference. Dive into our High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) for strength and stamina, or challenge yourself in our 1500 Sq. Ft. outdoor CrossFit area, TRX bands, and more.



Exclusive Amenities for a Luxurious Fitness Experience

Enjoy our 5-star amenities including new showers, steam rooms, changing areas with digital lockers, and a coffee cafe. We also offer convenient valet parking.


Join Our Community – Flexible Membership Options Available

Choose from a range of membership plans – from a single day to annual subscriptions. Every new member receives a complimentary Fitness Assessment, a Personal Training session, and a Body Composition Assessment.
